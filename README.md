ConnectX Telecom Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn data from a telecom company. The goal is to understand customer behavior, demographics, service usage, and other factors that may influence customers to leave the service.

Using data visualization techniques, the dashboard provides insights into customer churn patterns and helps identify factors affecting customer retention.

Problem Statement

The objective of this project is to analyze and visualize telecom customer churn data. The dashboard explores customer demographics, service usage, offers, contracts, and payment methods to identify patterns and factors that contribute to customer churn.

Dataset

The dataset used in this project contains telecom customer information including demographics, service usage, contract types, and revenue data.

Important Dataset Columns

Customer ID – Unique identifier for each customer

Gender – Gender of the customer

Age – Age of the customer

Number of Dependents – Number of individuals using the service under the same plan

City – Customer’s city

Zip Code – Customer’s postal code

Number of Referrals – Number of customers referred by the customer

Tenure in Months – Duration the customer has used the service

Offer – Promotional offer subscribed by the customer

Avg Monthly GB Download – Average data usage per month

Contract – Type of contract (Monthly, Yearly, etc.)

Payment Method – Method used to pay bills

Total Charges – Total charges billed to the customer

Total Refunds – Total refunded amount

Total Long Distance Charges – Charges for long-distance calls

Total Revenue – Total revenue generated from the customer

Customer Status – Indicates whether the customer has churned or is active

Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes important insights related to customer churn and telecom usage.

Visualizations Included

Churn Rate Visualization
Pie chart / Donut chart showing the percentage of customers who churned versus those who stayed.

Customer Demographics
Distribution of customers based on Age and Gender.

Customer Tenure Distribution
Histogram showing tenure in months with churned customers highlighted.

Contract Type Analysis
Bar chart showing the most popular contract types and churn comparison.

Payment Method Distribution
Chart showing the number of customers using each payment method.

Total Charges vs Tenure Relationship
Scatter plot showing the relationship between total charges and customer tenure with churned customers highlighted.

Total Revenue Distribution
Visualization showing how total revenue is distributed among customers.

Tools Used

Download the telecom churn dataset.

Analyze the insights related to customer churn and customer behavior.

Conclusion

This project helps in understanding the key factors influencing telecom customer churn. By analyzing customer demographics, service usage, contract types, and payment methods, businesses can develop strategies to improve customer retention and reduce churn.
